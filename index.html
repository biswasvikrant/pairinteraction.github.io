About

pairinteraction was originally developed and is currently maintained by the center for Rydberg Quantum Optics at the 5th Institute of Physics of the University of Stuttgart, Germany.

The software is written in C++ and Python. It is released fully open source under the terms of the Apache License 2.0. Binary installers are also made available for many system, in particular Microsoft Windows. Just follow the link in the sidebar.

Main Features

The pairinteraction software calculates pair potentials for Rydberg atoms in external fields. It supports a lot of neat features, the major of which are listed below:

  • Automatic selection rules
  • Stark and Zeeman maps for single atom states
  • Variable interatomic axis
  • Numerical and analytical methods for radial matrix elements
  • Multipole expansion to arbitrary order!

Some other convenient (non-physical) features are

  • Automatic fit for C3 and C6 coefficients
  • MATLAB compatible data export
  • Publication ready plots through PDF export
